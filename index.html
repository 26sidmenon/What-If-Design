ABOUT US

We are a team of Architecture graduates from IIT Kharagpur aiming to help people find the most appropriate design solutions to their products.

Having diverse corporate as well as studio experience enables us to provide a peripheral understanding of the products we build. What's better than working with designers who understand both consumer experience and business, right?
